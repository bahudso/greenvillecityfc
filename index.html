GREENVILLE CITY FOOTBALL CLUB

Greenville/Spartanburg area adult soccer & futsal team

ABOUT THE TEAM

Greenville City is a football club based in the Greenville/Spartanburg area. We play in adult leagues through Greenville Recreation and Upward Star Center.

Pictured to the left is our team from Upward Star Center's 2016 Futsal league in which we won season champions.

We are looking for new players to add to our teams for upcoming leagues, so please contact us if you are interested.
