GREENVILLE CITY FOOTBALL CLUB

Greenville/Spartanburg area adult soccer & futsal team

Looking to play soccer in the Greenville/Spartanburg area? Can't find a team? Don't know where to look? Open to joining other new/former players who want to play? We are here to help!

Greenville City FC started as a group of free agents, way back in 2002. Through the years our team has changed but one thing has remained consistent: We all love to play soccer! And we all want to see adult soccer grow in the Upstate.

So if you are looking to join a team, please contact us as soon as possible. We will help get you on a team. And it won't cost you a thing. We want to help grow adult soccer in this area, and we won't leave you hanging.

We can help you be a part of a team as soon as this summer! Summer 11v11 is organizing now!

ABOUT THE TEAM

Greenville City is a football club based in the Greenville/Spartanburg area. We play in adult leagues through the Upward Star Center.

Pictured to the left is our team from Upward Star Center's 2016 Futsal league in which we won season champions.

We are currently playing at the Upward Star Center, between Greenville and Spartanburg. Wednesday nights we play in an 11v11 league with a full field, full goals, and 3 referees. This is a new Rec league (Upward also has a Premier league for more advanced play). The field surface is artificial turf and is the best in the area - no uneven grass with divots or flooded with water. The games are played rain or shine (except thunder/lightning). Most people in Greenville think the Upward Center is too far of a drive - it is not. It is right off of 85, exit 66. To give a better idea of distance, 85 and 385 is exit 51 and 85 and Pelham is exit 54.

Other benefits of the Upward league is that there are no player cards. Each player on the roster creates an account with Upward and then joins their team, through a team code. When each player joins their team, they pay a team fee, significantly easing the load on the team captain who usually pays a large amount at once. All of the team members then pitch in and split the referee fees for the season. It's that simple. And not a pain... If there are any roster issues, there is always a field rep who can check the official roster against player identification.

Please contact us if you are ready to play!
